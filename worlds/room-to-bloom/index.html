Room to Bloom

A quieter kind of decorating game

Turn empty rooms into places worth living in — one considered choice at a time. Room to Bloom is a grown-up take on home decorating: real budgets, real trade-offs, and rooms that actually feel lived-in.

About
Design for someone else, on a budget

Every room comes with a client, a budget, and a brief — not just an empty box to fill. You shop a real catalog by category, place pieces by hand, and check in with your client as you go. There's no single correct layout to find; there's a budget to respect and a person to satisfy, and the two don't always agree.

What makes it different
Not casual, not dark — considered
💬Real client feedback
Your client reacts to what you've actually built — calling out when a room feels uniform, disconnected, or missing a clear mood — instead of scoring you against a hidden checklist.
💰A budget that bites
Every piece has a real price. A bigger plant or a nicer chair means something else doesn't make the cut — decorating is a series of trade-offs, not an unlimited shopping spree.
🗂️A full catalog, by category
Seating, lighting, plants, decor, and more, each browsable on its own — so you're choosing between options that make sense together, not scrolling one giant list.
🌿Rooms with a point of view
Warm and plant-filled, cool and uniform, sparse and quiet — the brief pushes toward a specific feeling, and the room either lands there or it doesn't.
